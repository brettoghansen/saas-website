Why It Works

Every business built a door and left it open. You're just walking through it.

Contact forms exist for one reason: so potential customers can reach the business. When you submit through one, your message arrives as a website inquiry — not email, not cold outreach. The business receives it through the same channel their best customers use.

Your message is a POST request to their own web server. There's no inbox to filter it, no domain reputation to destroy, no warming period.

02
