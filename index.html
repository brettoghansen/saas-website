Why It Works

Every business built an inbox for new inquiries.
Nobody's using it.

Every business publishes a contact form on their website — built for customers, vendors, and partners to reach them. It routes directly to the person who handles new leads. Not a spam folder. Not a promotions tab. Their actual inbox.

Salespeople occasionally fill these out manually. It works well. But nobody does it at scale — finding each form, filling every field, solving CAPTCHAs, and writing a message for 500 companies would take days.

Canvass does it automatically. Import your list, write your message once, and start the campaign. Canvass handles every form, every field, every CAPTCHA. The business gets a real website inquiry — because that's exactly what it is.

No spam filter. Website contact submissions route through the business's own site directly to their inbox.

No domain warmup. No infrastructure. No rotating IPs. Nothing to configure before you start.

They're expecting it. Businesses publish that form because they want to hear from people. You're using the channel they built for exactly this.

Under the Hood

The technology that makes it work
where simpler tools fail.

Intelligence

AI Form Detection

Most contact forms aren't standard. Unusual field labels, custom dropdowns, honeypot fields, hidden inputs. Claude AI reads the form and maps your information correctly — dramatically improving fill rate on non-standard layouts.

Reads field context, not just field names
Handles Gravity Forms, WPForms, CF7, Ninja Forms
Falls back to email if no form is found
Automation

CAPTCHA Auto-Solve

The #1 reason contact form outreach fails at scale is CAPTCHAs. Canvass integrates 2captcha to solve reCAPTCHA and hCaptcha automatically. Records that can't be solved silently route to your manual queue — nothing gets silently dropped.

reCAPTCHA v2, v3, and hCaptcha
~$1–3 per 1,000 solves
Unsolvable records flagged for manual review
Delivery

Email Fallback via SES

When a business has no contact form but lists an email address, Canvass sends directly via email. Integrated with Amazon SES — $0.10 per 1,000 emails, sent from your own domain, with better deliverability than shared Gmail infrastructure.

Sends from your own verified domain
$0.10/1,000 — not $400/month
